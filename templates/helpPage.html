Help Page

FastTask is a todo list app that allows you to easily keep track of your tasks and stay well organized. You can create tasks with the name of your choice, a deadline, a description and a reminder notification. You can also gain XP and level up by completing enough tasks and on the app page you can view your current level and XP.

To sign up and log in, just click on the big sign up button at the center of the main page or the login button at the top right corner of the page, to the left of the help button used to access this page from the main page.

To create a task, just click on the big plus button at the top left corner of the page below the header (purple FastTask name) and a modal box will pop up where you can enter the name, deadline, description and reminder date and time for the task. After creating the task, you can also edit or delete it by clicking on the task button with the title and deadline of the one you want to edit or delete, a similar modal box will pop up with the current information of this task. You can simply input new information of the task. To delete the task, just click on the delete button.

To mark a task as complete, just click on the complete button in the modal box that pops up when you click on the task button that has the title and deadline of the task you want to mark. You can get 20 XP for each completed task and when you reach 100 times your current level, you will level up and your XP will be reset to 0. For example, if you're currently at level 3, you will need to complete 15 tasks to level up assuming you have 0 XP.
